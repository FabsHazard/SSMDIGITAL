Welcome To SafeSatoshiMoon

The foundation of any successful cryptocurrency is their rigid community. Starting on Day 1, SafeSatoshiMoon holders have come together to collaborate, creating resources and a solid base for the community.


The SafeSatoshiMoon community started when a group of friends in the UK stumbled upon a new cryptocurrency where the developer had tried—and failed—to rug the holders. The evidence was there: the developer held 11% of the supply, but instead of transferring the rest of the supply to himself, he transferred his supply to the SSM contract address—effectively giving the entire supply of the token back to anyone who was willing to buy it.


The four friends built a Telegram group for the currency and started to spread the word, and in less than a week a community had come together to build a website, a social media presence, and a marketing team—and to start the process of getting listed on major cryptocurrency exchanges.


The community owns 100% of the coin supply, and by joining together we can help Satoshi land Safely on the Moon. On this journey, holders are rewarded with a 3% tax every time someone sells, increasing the holder’s bag once we land on the moon. 7% of each sale is also added to the liquidity pool.


This is only the beginning. We have a lineup of current projects and promotions designed to help the community grow.
